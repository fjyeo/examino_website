Introduction to Examino

Welcome to Examino, your AI-powered coaching assistant for track and field athletes. This documentation will guide you through the features and capabilities of our platform.

Examino uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze your performance, provide real-time feedback, and offer personalized tips to help you improve your technique and achieve your athletic goals.

Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with Examino in just a few steps:

  1. Sign up for an account on our registration page.
  2. Set up your athlete profile with your sport and goals.
  3. Upload your first performance video for analysis.
  4. Receive AI-powered feedback and recommendations.

For the best results, ensure your videos are shot from a stable position with a clear view of your full performance.

Account Setup

To get the most out of Examino, follow these steps to set up your account:

  1. Fill in your personal details, including age, height, and weight.
  2. Select your primary track and field events.
  3. Set your performance goals and target competitions.
  4. Connect your wearable devices for additional data tracking (optional).

A complete profile helps our AI provide more accurate and personalized feedback.

AI Analysis

Examino's core feature is its AI-powered analysis of your performance videos. Here's how it works:

  1. Our AI model analyzes your form, technique, and movements in uploaded videos.
  2. It compares your performance to optimal techniques and your previous performances.
  3. The AI generates detailed feedback on areas for improvement.
  4. You receive personalized tips and drills to enhance your performance.

Example Analysis Result

{
  "event": "long_jump",
  "overall_score": 8.2,
  "strengths": ["approach speed", "takeoff angle"],
  "areas_for_improvement": ["landing technique"],
  "recommended_drills": [
    "Box drill for landing",
    "Toe-tap exercises for foot placement"
  ]
}

Performance Tracking

Examino allows you to track your progress over time:

  • View historical data of your performances.
  • Track improvements in specific aspects of your technique.
  • Set and monitor personal bests for each event.
  • Analyze trends in your performance leading up to competitions.

Personalized Tips

Based on your performance analysis, Examino provides personalized tips to help you improve:

  • Technique adjustments specific to your event and current skill level.
  • Customized training plans to address areas needing improvement.
  • Nutrition and recovery advice tailored to your training intensity.
  • Mental preparation strategies for competition day.

Pro Tip: Regularly update your goals and competition schedule to receive the most relevant tips and advice.

Video Upload

To get the most accurate analysis, follow these guidelines when uploading videos:

  • Use a stable camera or tripod to minimize shakiness.
  • Ensure the entire performance area is in frame.
  • Record at 60fps or higher for detailed motion analysis.
  • Upload raw footage without edits or slow-motion effects.

Real-time Feedback

Examino's real-time feedback feature provides instant insights during your training sessions:

  • Connect your mobile device to our app during practice.
  • Receive audio cues for immediate technique adjustments.
  • View real-time metrics like approach speed or jump height.
  • Get instant comparisons to your personal bests.

Note: Real-time feedback is available for Premium and Enterprise users only.
